Atmel SAM CMSIS

CMSIS refers to the bare-metal programming of Atmel's ARM processors (ATSAM). CMSIS programming is basically just shifting 32-bit values into registers and hope that it works as intended. In my projects i used an ATSAM4S16C.

Animationtester

The Animationtester is a tool that i wrote to verify my 2D animations. You can alter the frequency to see which framreate suits your animation most. It uses Qt5 and runs on linux. The pallete of the widgets has been tweaked to fit perfectly into the Ubuntu Yaru theme.
